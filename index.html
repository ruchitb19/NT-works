WHO WE ARE

NT-works, is a modern IT services firm focused on building robust, reliable, and future-ready technology solutions. Driven by a passion for solving complex digital problems, our team brings deep expertise in software development, server administration, and enterprise-grade deployments.
We partner with clients to design, build, and scale solutions that meet real-world challenges—delivering performance, security, and long-term value every step of the way.

SERVICES WE PROVIDE

→ Software Development

Custom Java applications, web apps, and mobile-friendly solutions built with performance and security in mind.

→ Server Deployment & Management

Specializing in Tomcat, Apache, and RHEL environments, including WAR file deployment, SSL configuration, and troubleshooting.

→ Cybersecurity & Compliance

We help fix vulnerabilities, secure your server infrastructure, and implement industry-standard best practices.

→ IT Consulting

From architecture planning to digital transformation strategy, we guide businesses at every tech crossroad.

→ Maintenance & Support

Ongoing monitoring, bug fixes, system updates, and technical support so you can focus on your business.
